# hotepme 🕊️

*Offering-peace-as-received-ordinance — the pre-fragmentation root where the gift, the satisfaction, the contentment, and the peace are one event, not a price and its payout: Egyptian hotep carried as a decree.*

hotepme is two true roots welded into one word: the Egyptian **htp** (𓊵, *hotep*), the most remarkable sacrifice-word in any tongue — its hieroglyph a low altar bearing bread and libation, its meaning *offering, peace, satisfaction, contentment, rest* all at once — and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So hotepme is the offering held as an ordinance: not sacrifice → peace, but sacrifice IS peace, the giving and the rest folded into a single act. The canon notes that Hebrew **shelamim** (the peace-offering where God, priest, and offerer all share the meal) reached the same insight independently — "for all are complete in it."

hotepme names a **grade** of meaning, not one particular offering: the whole circuit closed as one event — the bread set down, the satisfaction shared, the order restored, the calm that fills the room. Its light is the wholeness modernity forgot it owned: it dissolves the merchant's logic at the altar and lets giving simply be rest. Its shadow is the costless counterfeit — wanting the peace and contentment without the offering — and its older twin, the offering wielded as a bribe. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.00/10**.
